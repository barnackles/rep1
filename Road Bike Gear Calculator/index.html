Cycling Gear Calculator

Using this simple app you can quickly calculate your gear ratios, speed and cadence.

  1. Choose number of teeth on your chainring

  2. Choose number of teeth on your cog

  3. Choose your wheel circumference

  4. Use cadence slider to calculate cycling speed on any chosen gear setup
